Our Mission

One place for every
Greek-owned business

All across the world, Greek immigrants have built restaurants, schools, churches, law firms, and so much more. The United States alone is home to approximately 3 million Greek-Americans — woven into their communities from coast to coast.

But there has never been a single, modern place to find and support these businesses. Until now. The Greek Directory is that place — a fast, reliable, and smart platform connecting our community with the businesses that belong to it.

Founder's Message

Why I built
The Greek Directory

Greek businesses are everywhere — but they're not easy to find. That's the problem I set out to solve.

I grew up in the suburbs of Chicago in a Greek-speaking environment and have been part of the community my entire life. But I started noticing something: outside of personal connections, Greek resources are scattered, outdated, or simply hidden.

My idea for The Greek Directory came from something simple. I was at church one Sunday, using the GOARCH Digital Chant Stand to follow the liturgy on my device. Afterward, people around me asked how I had found it so easily.

That moment made me ask: why isn't everything Greek-related this easy to find? Not just church readings — but businesses, organizations, events, churches. That's when this became more than an idea.

I reached out to business owners, restaurateurs, and entrepreneurs throughout Chicagoland. Their responses were consistent: "Greek businesses need better visibility. The community needs a central place to connect."

The Greek Directory is being built to be exactly that — a trusted, organized, and growing platform where anyone can discover and support Greek-owned businesses.

Since November 2025, I've been building this from the ground up, writing thousands of lines of code myself. Right now, this is a one-person operation, and to scale this properly, our community needs to be involved.

Absolutely. Claim your free listing at any time — no credit card required. When you're ready to upgrade, you can do so from your Business Portal with no data loss. Your listing history, photos, and contact info all carry over.
Featured placement rotates among Featured and Premium businesses within a given context (city, category, etc.), with a maximum of 12 slots. Premium listings always appear first in related sections. While exact position isn't guaranteed, Featured and Premium businesses consistently outperform Free in both visibility and click-through.
Premium listings can display a subtle auto-generated gradient derived from your brand color. It's applied only to the outer background of your listing page — all content cards remain clean white. You control the intensity (Subtle, Medium, or Strong) from your Business Portal.
No tier removes competitor listings — this maintains the integrity of the directory and user trust. What Premium does is ensure your business always appears first in related listing sections and map clusters, giving you a decisive head start.
Annual billing gives you 2 months free — roughly 17% off. Featured is $448/year (vs $348 at monthly) and Premium is $986/year (vs $948 at monthly). Your plan renews automatically each year, and you can cancel at any time.
The Greek Directory is specifically for Greek-owned businesses across America — restaurants, churches, schools, professional services, and more. If your business is owned or operated by someone of Greek heritage, you're welcome to list and become part of the community.

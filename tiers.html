Questions

Common questions

Is my listing always free?
Yes. Free listings have no expiration and remain in the directory indefinitely. You can claim and manage your listing at any time through the Business Portal.
Can I cancel my plan anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time and your listing reverts to Free at the end of the billing period. Annual plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled to prevent renewal.
How do I upgrade from Free?
Sign in to your Business Portal at thegreekdirectory.org/business, navigate to your listing, and select Upgrade. You'll be guided through the payment process.
What does "priority ranking" mean?
When visitors search or browse listings, Premium listings appear first, followed by Featured, then Free. This applies to search results, category pages, and the map view. Within each tier, proximity and relevance determine order.
My business has multiple locations — do I pay per location?
Each location is its own listing. Chain linking (the "More Locations" feature) is available on all tiers. Contact us if you manage multiple locations — we have bundle options available.

Hellenic American Academy

"Private dual-language day school & examination center for Ellinomatheia."

Hellenic American Academy is one of the largest Greek-American educational institutions in the United States. It offers a distinctive dual-language private day school program for students from Preschool through 8th Grade, preparing them for lifelong success. The Academy's problem-based learning curriculum emphasizes social, emotional, and academic growth and is fully recognized by the Illinois State Board of Education.

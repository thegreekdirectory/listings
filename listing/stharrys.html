About

We are an Orthodox Christian home, inviting all to experience transformation through a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. Rooted in the rich traditions of the Orthodox faith, we strive to create a welcoming and nurturing environment for all to grow in faith, love, and service to God and one another.
Our core values of Excellence, Responsibility, and Compassion guide us in all that we do:
  • Excellence: We are committed to the highest standards of worship, education, and service, striving to honor God in every aspect of our church life.
  • Responsibility: We embrace our call to be faithful stewards of God's gifts, taking responsibility for our spiritual growth, our community, and the world around us.
  • Compassion: We seek to reflect the love of Christ by offering kindness, care, and support to those in need, both within and beyond our parish.
Through these values, we aim to inspire all to live out their faith with purpose and dedication, growing together as one in Christ.

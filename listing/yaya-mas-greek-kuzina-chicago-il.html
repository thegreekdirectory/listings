Yaya Mas Greek Kuzina

Dishes telling stories of Greece’s rich culinary heritage.

Call Website Directions Menu & Order
Yaya Mas Greek Kuzina serves Chicago’s most authentic Greek food — hand-carved gyros, fresh baked bougatsa, avgolemono soup, spanakopita, tiropita, moussaka, pastitsio, Greek Pinsa and baklava. All ingredients imported directly from Greece. Available for pickup and delivery in Lincoln Park and across Chicago. Open until 5AM Wednesday through Saturday.

The Yaya Mas Greek Kuzina menu features authentic Greek food made fresh daily with imported Greek ingredients. Our menu includes hand-carved gyros and souvlaki, fresh baked bougatsa (vanilla custard pastry), spanakopita and tiropita, avgolemono egg-lemon soup, Greek Pinsa flatbreads, moussaka, pastitsio, Greek salads, baklava and our famous Freddo cappuccino. We also offer a full late night menu open until 5AM Wednesday through Saturday.

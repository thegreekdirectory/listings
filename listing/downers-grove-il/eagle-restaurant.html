Family Owned & Operated

Established in 1981, we are famous for our delicious American-style cuisine located in Downers Grove, IL.  Visit Eagle Restaurant today and enjoy our daily specials/value meals always destined to please not only your appetite, but your wallet too! Enjoy lunch & dinner in our cozy dining room or outdoor patio. We also accommodate catering packages for office parties & family gatherings.

History of Eagle

It all started back in 1981 when Lee Zografos, founder & owner, realized that potential of an abandoned gas station on the corner of Maple & Fairview in Downers Grove. He envisioned a Dine in / Carryout family operated restaurant that would be a neighborhood staple for generations to come.  So in the fall of 1981, Lee, with his brother-in-law Tassos opened Eagle Restaurant.  Over the years, with Lee mainly running his other restaurant, Town Kitchen in Clarendon Hills, both of their sons helped out working in the restaurant. In 2005, when Tassos retired, Lee's son John took over his partnership.
